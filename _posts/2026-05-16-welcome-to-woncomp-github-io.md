This is the first post in the new site.

The blog is built with `Jekyll`, themed with `Chirpy`, and deployed to `GitHub Pages`. Posts are written in Markdown and stored in `_posts/`.

## Suggested front matter

Use this structure as a starting point for future posts:

```yaml
---
title: Post Title
date: 2026-05-16 16:00:00 +0800
categories: [top-category, sub-category]
tags: [tag-one, tag-two]
description: Short summary for previews and SEO.
---
```
